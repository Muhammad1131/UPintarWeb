U-PINTAR SECURE WEB ACCESS

 

Welcome to the U PINTAR website. Since its establishment, U PINTAR, until now, is still active in providing education and care facilities for children around Selangor and the Federal Territory. The segregation of children in and at each branch premises, is according to age level to ensure effective development in every activity that is carried out also has a positive impact on the overall development of children. This is to ensure that the quality of service and superior care is implemented. Guided by the Islamic learning system, the application of the basics of Islamic education such as memorization of common surahs, hadiths and daily prayer recitations as well as the Prophet's sunnah are emphasized, thus making parenting and education easier and more effective. We at U Pintar educate our foster children so that they can be independent and self-identified, wise in managing their time and clever with skills, adept at communicating and interacting with peers and people grown up with manners. It is always our intention and prayer that the children of U Pintar become pious, knowledgeable and excellent individuals.
