Frequently Asked Questions

What is U-Pintar Kindergarten?

U-Pintar Kindergarten is a nurturing and educational environment where children can grow, learn, and play. Our curriculum focuses on holistic development through fun and engaging activities.

What age group do you accept?

We accept children aged 3 to 6 years old. Our programs are tailored to meet the developmental needs of each age group.

How do I enroll my child?

Enrollment can be done through our website or by visiting our kindergarten. Please fill out the enrollment form to secure your child's spot.

What is the daily schedule like?

Our daily schedule includes a mix of structured activities, playtime, and snack breaks, ensuring a balanced day for the children.

Are meals provided?

Yes, we provide nutritious meals and snacks to ensure the children have the energy they need to thrive throughout the day.
