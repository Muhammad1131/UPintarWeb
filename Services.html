SERVICES

TADIKA ISLAM UNI PINTAR (5-6 tahun)

Uni Pintar Islamic Kindergarten is open to children aged 5 and 6 years. Our program nurtures independence and resilience. By primary school, students will have learned to read, count, write, and more.

  • Hours: 8 am to 12:30 pm
  • Basic Subjects: Malaysian, English, Mathematics, Science, Physical Education, Creative Art, Speed Reading Class, Storytelling
  • Islamic Education:
    • Arabic
    • Al-Quran
    • Iqra
    • Practice praying 5 times a day
    • Memorization of daily prayers
    • Verse memorization
    • Jawi

TASKA U PINTAR (3-4 tahun)

Our nursery program builds social skills, early math skills, and literacy for children aged 3-4. The curriculum includes group activities aligned with the PERMATA Negara curriculum.

  • SMART Programs:
    • Study Smart
    • Kedai Pintar
    • Smart Kitchen
    • Smart Reading
    • Smart Personality
    • Solat Pintar
    • Nasyid Pintar

TAMAN ASUHAN U PINTAR (0-2 tahun)

Providing daycare services from 6:30 am to 6:30 pm, including meal provision, hygiene management, and thematic activities. Services focus on early development as defined by the Social Welfare Department.

TRANSIT U PINTAR

Transit services for primary school children, including prayer times, study sessions, reading, and playtime with building games like LEGO.
